FAQ

  • I am unable to connect to the server due to a weird looking error message.
    You have probably fallen victim to one of the mysterious bugs in the Minecraft client. Try connecting a few more times. If you have no luck, restart your client and try again. This typically means there is a problem with the Minecraft authentication servers. Also, you will not be able to connect unless you have registered.
  • When walking around or after logging in, I see huge square holes in the ground.
    The big holes you see are "chunks" that didn't load. Chunks are the server's basic unit for mass-loading/transferring blocks. Sometimes they don't load correctly. If the hole doesn't disappear eventually, logging out and logging back in usually fixes it.
  • The Mac client does X weird thing.
    The Mac client is a bit more buggy as of Minecraft 1.5, in comparison to the other platforms. Sorry.
