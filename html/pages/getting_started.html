Getting Started

Note: If you have not registered (via a trial membership or by paying dues), this page will not be of any use to you, as you won't be able to connect to the server. See the Registration page.

This is a brief, down-and-dirty guide to getting started on our humble Minecraft server. We assume that you have been white-listed at this point, which means you are either using a trial membership, or have paid dues.

Watch the Tutorial first

We strongly recommend watching this great tutorial video on Youtube before logging in, to avoid unecessary death/confusion. Take ten minutes and give it a watch if you don't already know what you're doing.

Connecting to the Server

  • Open the Minecraft client, and you'll find yourself looking at a connect screen.
  • Enter the same username/password you did when registering on minecraft.net when you were buying the game. You can then hit Login, and Minecraft will auto-update and dump you into another menu screen.
  • Click on the Multiplayer button. You should now have a text box to input the following server address: smp.creeperslament.com
  • Hit the Connect button, and with any luck, you'll soon find yourself in front of town hall.

Where to go from here

After connecting, you may explore the starter town, and make your way out to the wilderness to start building when ready. Please do not break or build immediately around or within the starter town.

A good way to pick out somewhere to build your first house is by looking around on our world map

Assorted Tips

  • Skim over our Useful Resources page. The crafting stuff in particular is extremely useful.
  • If you have not already, watch the tutorial on Youtube or you will be lost.
  • You can talk to other players by pressing T, typing what you want to say, then hitting Enter.
  • Watch out for Spiders, Skeletons, Zombies, or Creepers. Creepers (the green guys with no arms) in particular are dangerous, as they run up to you and suicide explode themselves in your face.
  • Monsters appear at night in places that aren't well-lit with torches. You probably want to seek shelter at night.
  • Iron and Diamonds are more common at lower depths. Diamonds are best found at the 12 Y-coordinate. You can see your current coordinates by hitting the F3 key.
  • If you die, you will re-appear at your spawn point. If you have created and slept in a bed, your spawn point is said bed. If you have not built/used a bed, you will re-appear in front of town hall.
