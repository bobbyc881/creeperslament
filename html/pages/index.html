Survival Multiplayer Minecraft at its best

Creeper's Lament is a survival multiplayer (SMP) Minecraft server. We are semi-private in that we only allow players in that have registered, but we impose no limits on who may do so. While we are a survival server, our playerbase gravitates towards building and exploration. This server is currently home to around ten active players.

What is this Minecraft thing?

Minecraft is low-fi building, exploration, and survival game. It is extremely simple, a great creative outlet, and playable even by those who don't consider themselves gamers.

The rough premise is that you run around gathering resources and building materials, and set out to construct your very own castles, houses, bridges, subways, floating islands, statues, or whatever else you can imagine. Meanwhile, you have to avoid monsters (that's where the survival element comes into play). Youtube videos, screenshots, and write-ups don't adequately show why this game is fun, it's something that makes a lot more sense after trying it.

You may buy the game for about $25. The developer is a small indie game company, so queue feel-good feelings for supporting a budding company with great ideas.

Some Background Info

This server was started as a result of a handful of friends being frustrated with a few servers we haunted. Many of the original members of Creeper's Remorse are professionals in Web development, Networking, and the IT field at large. Combining our knowledge and experience, we aim to deliver the following:

  • Reliability. It sucks when someone gets in the way of your addic... hobby. The server only goes down for patching when new goodies are released.
  • Performance. This is not being ran on a spare machine in someone's basement. We are hosted in a high grade data center in Atlanta, and are able to scale up to handle more players as needed.
  • Safety. We back up aggressively, and are a white-list restricted server. Your creations will be safe, and should you choose to leave, feel free to take a copy of the latest world map with you.
  • Freedom from meddling. There are no in-game ops. Everyone must harvest, mine, and gather on their own. No freebies, no power-tripping mods. Changes and policies are discussed on our friendly mailing list.
